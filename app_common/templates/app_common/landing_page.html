Our Mission

▾

At Rational Education and Computer Training, our mission is to empower individuals of all ages and backgrounds with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in today's rapidly evolving digital landscape. Through comprehensive and accessible education, we aim to bridge the gap between aspiration and achievement, nurturing a community of lifelong learners equipped to thrive in a technology-driven world.

Our Vision

▾

Our vision at Rational Education and Computer Training is to be a leading institution of excellence in computer education and vocational training, recognized for our commitment to innovation, quality, and inclusivity. We aspire to cultivate a dynamic learning environment where creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration flourish, enabling our students to unleash their full potential and make meaningful contributions to society.
