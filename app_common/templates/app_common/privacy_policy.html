Privacy Policy

At React Institute, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy of our students, parents, and staff. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information
We may collect the following personal information from students, parents, and staff: Full name, address, phone number, email address, and date of birth. Academic records, attendance data, and examination results. Identification documents (e.g., ID cards, passports) for admission and verification. Financial details, such as payment information for fees and scholarships. Photographs and video recordings for identification, security, or promotional purposes.
1.2 Usage Data
We may collect information related to your interactions with our website, learning platforms, and digital resources, including: IP addresses and browser information. Login credentials and activity logs. Online communication data, such as emails or chat messages.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:
2.1 Academic and Administrative Functions
Processing admissions and maintaining student records. Managing attendance, grading, and progress reports. Facilitating communication with students and parents regarding institute-related activities.
2.2 Financial Transactions
Collecting tuition fees, fines, and other charges. Managing scholarships and financial aid programs.
2.3 Safety and Security
Ensuring the safety of students, staff, and visitors on campus. Monitoring access to institute facilities using ID cards or biometric systems.
2.4 Marketing and Communication
Promoting achievements, events, and activities of the institute via newsletters, brochures, and social media. Sending updates regarding courses, policies, or upcoming events.
2.5 Legal Compliance
Complying with legal obligations and responding to lawful requests from government authorities.
