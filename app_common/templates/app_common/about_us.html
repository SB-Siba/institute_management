About Us

Welcome to Treeranga!

At Treeranga, we believe that a t-shirt is more than just a piece of clothing – it's a canvas for self-expression, a symbol of comfort, and a statement of style. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create high-quality, unique t-shirts that resonate with our customers' personalities and lifestyles.

Our Story

Founded in 2022, Treeranga was born out of a passion for creativity and a love for fashion. Our founder, Sushant Kumar Jena, started the company with a small collection of hand-designed t-shirts, each one inspired by a unique story or concept. What started as a small endeavour quickly grew, thanks to the overwhelming support and enthusiasm from our community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring you the best t-shirts that combine style, comfort, and individuality. We believe that everyone deserves to feel good in what they wear, and we strive to offer a wide range of designs that cater to all tastes and preferences. From bold graphics to subtle prints, our collections are designed to inspire and empower.

Quality and Sustainability

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. We take pride in using only the finest materials to ensure that our t-shirts are not only stylish but also durable and comfortable. Moreover, we are committed to sustainability. We source our fabrics responsibly and work with manufacturers who share our values of ethical and sustainable practices.
